About Us

TechnoSoft is a technology company that develops, sells, repairs, and supports computers and related network, security products and services.

TechnoSoft sells personal computers, servers, data storage devices, network switches, software, computer peripherals, cameras, printers, photocopiers, land telephone sets, connectors, PAVA system, Queue management system, cable assemblies and electronics built by other manufacturers. The company is well known for its innovations in supply chain management and electronic commerce, particularly its direct-sales model and its "build-to-order" or "configure to order" approach to manufacturingâ€”delivering individual PCs configured to customer specifications. TechnoSoft is a pure hardware vendor for much of its existence, but it entered the market for IT services. The company has since made additional drive in storage and networking systems, with the aim of expanding their portfolio from offering computers only to delivering complete solutions for enterprise customers.

TechnoSoft has emerged as a one of the promising ICT distribution companies trading with hardware, networking and security system goods in Bangladesh. TechnoSoft has applied to be the authorized partner or distributor of different renowned world ICT brands, such as Hewlett Packard, Wisenet, HIK Vision, Oracle, Cisco, Canon, Toshiba, Telco Bridge, Dialogic, Dell, Avita, Epson, Fanvil, Grand Stream, CommScope, Lenovo, Oracle, Prolink, Samsung, etc. Many more brand companies are on our card to becoming partner or distributor soon
